Subject: 🔔 Coming Soon: Map Based Routing

*Mapping the Way: Visual Routing is Finally Here!*

Managing bulk orders just got a major visual upgrade. We are thrilled to introduce **Map-Based Routing**, a brand-new way to group and allocate your unrouted orders directly from an interactive map interface. No more guessing geometries or squinting at tabular lists — just clear visibility and smarter ad-hoc dispatching.

**Why We Built This?**

While our auto-allocation logic handles various scenarios seamlessly, some businesses require manual routing due to very specific on-ground SOPs.

Navigation — Orders > Pidge Powered > Map View (top right corner)

**What's New!**

1. **Forward Orders/Reverse Orders:** Pinpoints single-hub pickup locations alongside multiple drop destinations, featuring a left-hand summary panel of order counts per hub and a quick-reset map button for both forward and reverse orders. This view shows all pickup points for unprocessed forward orders, and displays all associated drop locations after clicking a selected pickup point icon.

2. **Hover Insights:** Hovering over any map pin instantly highlights essential delivery metrics, including delivery/pickup dates, promised windows, and operational wait times.

3. **On-the-Fly Route Construction:** Add orders to a route sequentially with a built-in call-to-action. Watch your delivery sequence connect in real-time with visual routing lines, rearrange legs dynamically, and assign everything to a Captive Rider seamlessly.

**What This Means To You**

1. **Zero Visibility Gaps:** You can now see your delivery clusters and bundle nearby orders efficiently as per custom SOPs.

2. **Ad-Hoc Efficiency:** Spot an active rider near a fresh pickup location? Allocate orders on the fly without bouncing back and forth into the live tracking dashboard. Reduce rider transit distances, cut fuel costs, and drastically accelerate your delivery turnarounds.
