Subject: 💥⚡️ What's New: One ID to track Everything

*One ID to Track Them All!*

To improve tracking consistency across the platform, we have updated the primary identifiers used on our dashboard.

In most of the places, different sort of ID's has been used as the primary tracking identifier. This difference often caused confusion for Admins to track orders. To eliminate this inconsistency and streamline your workflow, **Reference ID** has now been established as the universal primary identifier across everywhere.

**Why We Built This?**

We have standardized how orders are identified and displayed across both the **All Orders** and **Pidge Powered** sections. By making the **Reference ID** your main universal anchor, you no longer have to mentally translate between different IDs when tracking the same order across different tabs.

**What's New!**

1. **The New Universal Primary Key:** **Reference ID** is now officially the first, fixed, and non-editable column across all **Pidge Powered** pages (Overview, Ready to Ship, On Ground, At Warehouse, and Completed).

2. **Clearer Labeling:** We've renamed all instances of "Ref ID" and "Channel/Ref ID" to a clean, uniform **"Reference ID"** across all sub-tabs and the Order Details screen.

3. **Intelligent Date & Time Tracking:**
   - **Reference ID** timestamps now reflect the exact **Order Creation** date and time with Pidge.
   - **Order ID** timestamps will show the **Order Manifestation** date and time.

**What This Means To You**

1. **Zero Confusion, Faster Tracking:** No more switching between sections and seeing "Ref ID" in one place and "Order ID" in another. A single, consistent primary identifier means less cognitive load and faster tracking for Admins.

2. **Tailored Workspace Control:** By making Order ID optional and giving you a standardized starting column, your workspace becomes cleaner, more predictable, and easier to scan at a glance.
